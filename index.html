CSS coverage

One typical workflow in my team is the development of a thing, the adoptance of said thing and a subsequent turn down or deprecation. Typically in the deprecation phase various code paths are removed and space is reclaimed. This is super useful as bloat in our JavaScript or C++ can cause load times (and sometimes lead to various tricky bugs). An area that is often missed during this deprecation phase is the identification of CSS rules which will be orphaned by the removal of all this code....
